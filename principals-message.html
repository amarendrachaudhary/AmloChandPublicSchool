Principal's Message

At Amol Chand Public School, we are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals through a balanced focus on academics, values, and co-curricular development. Our experienced teachers, modern facilities, and caring environment create a space where students grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

We believe education should be a joyful journey. Our approach encourages curiosity, confidence, and creativity through experiential learning. We aim to instill strong values alongside academic excellence, preparing students to adapt, lead, and contribute meaningfully in a rapidly changing world.

We invite you to visit our campus and experience the positive and enriching atmosphere that defines our school.

Warm regards,
Mr. Anoop Agrawal
Principal
Amol Chand Public School, Hathras
