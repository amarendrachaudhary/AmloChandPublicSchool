üìö Academics & Curriculum Details
Pre-Primary Curriculum

Our Pre-Primary section focuses on developing foundational skills through fun, play-way methods, storytelling, art, and music to ensure holistic development of young learners.

Primary Curriculum (Class 1 - 5)

The Primary curriculum builds language, numeracy, science, and social understanding. Emphasis is placed on reading, writing, arithmetic, and basic life skills through activity-based learning.

Middle Curriculum (Class 6 - 8)

Subjects like Mathematics, Science, English, Social Science, and Computer Science form the core. Focus is on conceptual clarity, critical thinking, and project-based learning to prepare students for higher classes.

Senior Secondary Curriculum (Class 9 - 12)

Students are offered streams like Science, Commerce, and Humanities based on their interests and aptitude. Our aim is to prepare them for board exams, competitive exams, and future career paths with expert guidance and academic excellence.

üè´ Facilities

Library
Library

Our Library is a hub of knowledge, equipped with a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources. It provides students with a peaceful environment for study, research, and self-learning, fostering reading habits and academic excellence.

Computer Lab
Computer Lab

Our Computer Lab is equipped with modern computers, high-speed internet, and the latest software to promote digital literacy. Students gain practical experience in coding, research, and technology-driven learning, preparing them for the digital world.

Science Lab
Science Lab

Our Science Lab is equipped with modern tools and equipment, providing students with the opportunity to perform experiments and explore scientific concepts practically. It fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and innovation in a safe and structured environment.

Digital Class
Digital Classrooms

Our Digital Classrooms are equipped with interactive boards, multimedia tools, and advanced technology to enhance learning. This smart infrastructure enables engaging lessons, visual presentations, and interactive sessions, making education more effective and enjoyable for students.

Hostel
Hostel Facility

Our Hostel facility provides a safe, comfortable, and homely environment for students. With separate accommodations for boys and girls, the hostel offers nutritious meals, 24/7 security, and all essential amenities to support academic success and personal growth.

Mobile Maths Lab
Mobile Maths Lab

The Mobile Maths Lab brings mathematical concepts to life through hands-on activities and interactive tools. Designed to make learning mathematics engaging and fun, the lab travels to different classrooms, promoting logical thinking, problem-solving skills, and real-world applications.

Mobile Science Lab
Mobile Science Lab

The Mobile Science Lab brings hands-on scientific experiments directly to students, enhancing curiosity and innovation. Equipped with essential tools and interactive setups, this facility ensures that scientific learning is accessible even in remote or resource-constrained areas.

Our Infrastructure
Our Infrastructure

Our Infrastructure is designed to support holistic education with state-of-the-art classrooms, modern labs, playgrounds, auditoriums, and other essential facilities. The campus is spacious, secure, and equipped to foster academic excellence and co-curricular development.

Security and Infra
Security and Infrastructure

Our institution maintains top-class security with 24/7 surveillance, secured entry points, and trained personnel. The infrastructure includes modern classrooms, safety measures, and emergency protocols, ensuring a secure and comfortable learning environment for all students.

Sick Room
Sick Room

The Sick Room is equipped to handle minor health issues and emergencies that arise during school hours. A trained medical attendant and basic first-aid facilities ensure student well-being while parents are informed promptly when required.

Transport System
Transport System

Our Transport System ensures safe and reliable conveyance for students. Modern, GPS-enabled vehicles with trained staff provide hassle-free pick-up and drop-off facilities, covering urban and rural areas for the convenience of parents and students.

Atal-Tinkering-Lab
Atal Tinkering Lab

Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL) is an initiative to foster curiosity, creativity, and imagination in young minds. Equipped with modern tools, the ATL encourages students to innovate and experiment with technology, promoting hands-on learning and STEM education.
