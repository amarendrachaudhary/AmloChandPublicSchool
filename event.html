EVENTS - CELEBRATIONS

The school strongly believes that textbook learning is just one aspect of a child’s overall growth. To support holistic development, it offers a wide range of in-house activities. These activities and events are thoughtfully designed to nurture a well-rounded personality by fostering physical, practical, aesthetic, moral, and intellectual growth.
The school’s celebration programs are rooted in the idea of encouraging students to express their talents in diverse ways, making the learning experience both enjoyable and enriching. These celebrations include special assemblies, the annual function, and the sports meet. All dates and timings for these events are clearly outlined in the school calendar.
