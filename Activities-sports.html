Activities-Sports

Sports and games are an essential and mandatory component of the educational journey. They play a vital role in shaping character and instilling discipline. Participation in sports fosters respect for rules and regulations while teaching individuals the significance of self-control and perseverance.
Moreover, it promotes teamwork, resilience, and a spirit of fair play among students.

  • Volley Ball court

  • Table Tennis
